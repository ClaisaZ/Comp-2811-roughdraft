- Color 

Additive and Subtractive colors

Subtractive 
- White is every color in the spectrum
- A piece of white paper reflects every color in the spectrum
- If you draw something on the page (e.g red marker) you are subtracting/removing some f the spectru,

Additive
- Start with black
- Red Green Blue are primary colors
    - Secondary colors are
        - Cyan
        - Magenta
        - Yellow
        - Black 

Color Harmonies
- For your high fidelity wireframe you are required to:
    - provide a three-color color scheme
        - provide most used
        - secondary less used but still prominent
        - highlight sparingly used contrast color
    - the three color scheme CANNOT just inclue white and black
    - provide the color harmony you used
        - you cannot use analagous or monochromatic
    - pick two fonts
        - one heading font
        - one body font
    - IMPORTANT: your color scheme must validate at least AA for contrast use this [Contrast Checker](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/) to test your foreground and background colors

Color and branding
- choose a color that conveys something to your users
- targeted towards your intended audience
